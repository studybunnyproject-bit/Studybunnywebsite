What is Carrot Currency (CC)? 🥕

CC (Carrot Currency) is the reward system in your productivity app. You earn CC by completing tasks and using features like notes, Pomodoro, flashcards, and quizzes.

🥕 How to Earn CC:

  • ✅ Finish 10 to-do list tasks = +0.1 CC
  • 📝 Write 50 words in notes = +0.1 CC
  • ⏲️ Complete 10 minutes of Pomodoro = +0.1 CC
  • 🃏 10 correct flashcards = +0.1 CC
  • 🧠 10 correct quizzes = +0.1 CC

⚠️ You can lose CC if you’re inactive:

  • 📅 Miss 3 days in a row = -0.3 CC
  • 📅 Miss a whole week = -0.7 CC

💰 Want more CC? You can buy it:

  • $0.99 = 100 CC
  • $9.99 = 1500 CC
  • $19.99 = 4000 CC

Purchases help support the app and unlock special outfits and features!
